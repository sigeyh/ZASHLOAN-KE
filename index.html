Who Can Apply?

You qualify for a ZashLoan if you:

  • Are a Kenyan citizen aged 18 and above
  • Own a mobile phone with M-Pesa access
  • Have a valid national ID
  • Have no default with other mobile loan apps

Frequently Asked Questions

How much can I borrow?

You can borrow from Ksh 500 to Ksh 50,000 depending on your limit.

How long does it take?

Loan approval takes under 6 minutes once you register and apply.

Where does the money go?

The funds are deposited directly into your M-Pesa account.

Do I need security?

No. Our loans are unsecured – no collateral required.
